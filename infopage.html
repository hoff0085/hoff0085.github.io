Quokka Info


Species

Setonix brachyurus

Size

2.5 to 5 kg; 40 to 54cm long. Similar in size to a common house cat.

Habitat

South west Western Australia and coastal islands

Diet

Herbivore, favouring leafs, small shrubs, and grasses

Animal nature

Generally nocturnal, very friendly to humans and animals alike.

Population

Current population estimates for the Quokka are between 7,850 and 17,150. It is likely that the current population is declining.

Reason for threatened status

Quokkas are threatened due to habitat destruction and environmental factors such as drought and wildfires.
